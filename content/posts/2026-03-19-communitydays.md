**Data Science KC is teaming up with KC Data Professionals to bring a data track to CommunityDays 2026!**

## 🎤 Call for Speakers: CommunityDays Data Track

Data Science KC is partnering with the [KC Data Professionals group](https://kcdataprofessionals.org/) to assemble a series of data-related talks for [CommunityDays](https://www.communitydays.info/), a two-day conference happening **May 28-29, 2026**.

If you have a talk idea related to data science, analytics, engineering, machine learning, or any data-adjacent topic, we'd love to see your proposal. Big enterprise deployments, small pipelines you've hacked 'til it works, academic research, side projects, business-focused or deep in the technical details &mdash; all are welcome.

<span style="font-size:1.4em;">[**📝 Submit your proposal at communitydays.info!**](https://www.communitydays.info/)</span>

- **📅 Conference Dates:** May 28-29, 2026
- **⏰ Proposal Deadline:** March 31, 2026 _(please let us know if you need more time!)_
- **🎟️ Tickets:** Speakers get free entry, early bird tickets are $60 for the two-day conference. See [CommunityDays](https://www.communitydays.info/) to register.
